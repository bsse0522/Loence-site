Loence ERP Modules

Formation View Details
What Loence ERP "Formation" Module Provides You
MANAGING BRANCHES
  • Organize your business into sections.
  • Manage branches of your company as an administrator.
  • Expand your operation to unlimited locations.
MANAGING UNITS
  • Create unit and define its type of any branch.
  • Manage and observe different units of your company from one point.
  • Update any unit if needed.
Human Resource Management View Details
What Loence ERP "Human Resource Management" Module Provides You
DASHBOARD
  • Get a summary of employee chart using graphical demonstration.
  • Summarize attendance with bar diagram.
  • Review payroll summary using pie-chart.
MANAGING EMPLOYEE INFORMATION
  • Manage employee details i.e. basic information, personal information, office information, contact information, photo and others of every employee.
  • Appoint employees to particular branches.
  • Assign employee code and ID card number.
  • Award employees by promoting.
  • Transfer employees to other branches changing their roles.
  • Revoke or terminate their employment.
AUTOMATED ATTENDANCE TRACKING & ACCESS CONTROL
  • Keep track of presence, overtime, leave, penalty and work on holidays of every employee manually or automatically by using attendance system such as fingerprint sensor and RFID scanner.
  • Manually input the attendance or shift attendance.
  • Add toil and overtime.
  • Grant leave of any employee if he or she applies for it.
  • Get attendance calendar of any employee.
GENERATING PAYROLL SHEET
  • Manage salaries of every employee using payroll feature.
  • Add bonus and overtime money to salaries or deduct penalty money due to absence or leave.
  • Keep track of depositing remuneration money to their respective accounts or employees taking their salaries from accountant.
MANAGING HR LOANS
  • Maintain date of taking loan, the amount of loan and returned money and process of returning money.
  • Dismiss the loan if it has been paid.
MANAGING HOLIDAYS
  • Maintain your company’s official calendar by assigning holidays and work days.
  • Add any event to a particular date.
Accounting View Details
What Loence ERP "Accounting" Module Provides You
ILLUSTRATIVE AND ANALYTIC ACCOUNTING REPORT
  • Review the amount of asset using graphical demonstration.
  • Analyze the amount of liabilities and owner’s equity using pie-chart.
  • Evaluate the amount of cash flow with graphical demonstration.
GENERAL JOURNAL
  • Record any economic event, reduce errors with pre-configured consistency checks and apply automatic debit and credit rules for segregate accounts. Most of the events are journalized automatically.
  • For exceptional economic events or adjusting entries an interactive page is offered to journalize the transactions.
JOURNALIZING ADJUSTING ENTRIES
  • Create a journal based on the accounts affected by the transaction.
  • Associate any journal with its respective employee, supplier or customer.
  • Submit only when credit and debit amount is same.
CHART OF ACCOUNTS
  • Classify them according to your preference and label them based on the account number.
  • Filter accounts into five categories i.e. asset, liabilities, owner’s equity, revenue and expense.
  • Go through the details of any ledger.
MANAGING ASSET
  • Maintain assets based on the accounts and branches they belong to.
  • List economic resources with its supplier, revaluation (depreciation or appreciation) with its following curve and its current value.
  • Dispose any asset anytime with options of gaining or losing on sale.
MANAGING LIABILITIES
  • Manage liabilities account with receiver’s name, type of the receiver and the amount of payment.
  • In case of payment not paying right away, the rest of the payment will be automatically transferred to liabilities account with an option to pay using cash, cheque or LC.
MANAGING RECEIVABLES
  • Control your receivables account with payer’s name, type of the payer and the amount of cash you would receive.
  • The rest of the payment will be automatically transferred to receivables account with an option to receive using cash, cheque or LC, in case payment of any transaction is not received right away.
Financial Statements View Details
What Loence ERP "Financial Statements" Module Provides You
INCOME STATEMENT
  • Record a financial statement that reports a company's financial performance over a specific accounting period.
  • Assess financial summary by giving a summary of how the business incurs its revenues and expenses through both operating and non-operating activities.
BALAMCE SHEET
  • Find a statement of the assets, liabilities, and capital of a business or other organization at a particular point in time, detailing the balance of income and expenditure over the preceding period.
OWNER’S EQUITY STATEMENT
  • Portray changes in the capital balance of a business over a reporting period by statement of owner’s equity.
  • The amount of owner's equity is increased by income and owner contributions. The balance is decreased by losses and owner draws.
